what is data analysis?

Data analysis is the systematic process of collecting, cleaning, transforming, and modeling data to discover useful information, derive insights, and support decision-making.

It converts raw data into actionable intelligence to identify patterns, evaluate performance, and predict future trends, combining statistics and business intelligence.
